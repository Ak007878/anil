Nellore City

Nellore is a city on the banks of Penna River,[3] located in Nellore district of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh.[4] Located 700 km southwest of state's executive capital Visakhapatnam. It serves as the headquarters of the district, as well as Nellore mandal and Nellore revenue division.[5] It is the fourth most populous city in the state. It is at a distance of 280 km from the Vijayawada and about 170 km north of Chennai, Tamilnadu and also about 380km east-northeast of Bangalore, Karnataka. Nellore City accounts for 65% of the urban population of Nellore District.
